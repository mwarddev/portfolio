ABOUT ME

Martin Ward

Junior Full Stack Software Developer

  • I'm Martin, a coffee fueled Junior Full Stack Software Developer from the East of England
  • I have a fascination with all things tech (hardware and software)
  • I'm an analytical problem solver
  • I'm an advocate of using tech to automate tasks and make life easier
  • I love to learn new skills and build upon existing ones

Working in manufacturing since high school, I worked my way up to Factory Manager, earning a Diploma in Management and developing a plethora of transferable soft skills.

In 2020 I discovered a passion for coding and self taught for a year, learning every day and experimenting with different coding languages (not just printing "Hello World"). I discovered Python was the language that made everything click for me.

I enjoyed coding so much that in 2021 I enrolled in a year-long Full Stack Software Development course with Code Institute and fully committed to changing my career to one in tech.

Learning the Django tech stack and more besides, I graduated the course with a diploma at Merit grade, obtained a range of new skills, and created 5 portfolio projects.

Whilst working on the course I also took part in a Retro Gaming Hackathon and learnt what it's like to work in a team environment.
